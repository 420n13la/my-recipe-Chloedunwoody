Martha Stewart's Famous Apple Crisp

Introduction

One of the definitive desserts of fall, this apple crisp recipe is easy and economical.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 tablespoons (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tablesppons white sugar
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3 pounds apples (Empire or Gala) cut into chuncks
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps for crumble

  1. Preheat oven to 375℉
  2. In a large bowl, mix together flour, brown sugar, salt and 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  3. Using a blender, cut butter into flour until mixture is a coarse meal
  4. Transfer to large bowl, add oats and combine using your hands
  5. Transfer the bowl to the freezer while preparing the apples

Steps for apple mixture

  1. In another large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice, cinamon, and remaining 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  2. Transfer mixture to a shallow 2-quart baking dish
  3. Sprinkle the crumble mixture on top of the apples
  4. Place dish in oven and cook for 55 to 65 minutes
